TagPass Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

TagPass respects your privacy.

Information Collection

TagPass does not require you to create an account. TagPass does not collect personal information for its own use.

Camera Access

TagPass requests access to your device camera so you can scan barcodes and QR codes. Camera data is used only for scanning passes inside the app.

Stored Data

Passes you scan or create in TagPass are stored locally on your device. This may include names you assign to passes, barcode or QR code contents, and optional notes you enter.

Data Sharing

TagPass does not sell your data. TagPass does not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or tracking.

Tracking

TagPass does not track you across apps or websites.

Analytics

TagPass does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Deletion

You can delete saved passes at any time from within the app. Removing the app from your device will also remove its locally stored data, subject to normal iOS device backup behavior.

Children’s Privacy

TagPass is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact Brandon White via GitHub.
